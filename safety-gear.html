Why High Visibility Matters

Visibility is one of the most important factors in bicycle safety. Whether you're riding in bright daylight, low‑light conditions, or harsh weather, being seen by drivers, pedestrians, and other cyclists dramatically reduces the risk of collisions.

High‑visibility gear—especially neon colors and reflective materials—can increase your visibility by up to 500% in low‑light conditions.
