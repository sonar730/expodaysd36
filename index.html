Why 21-speed bicycles?

A 21-speed setup gives you a wide range of gears without overwhelming complexity. It’s versatile enough for hills, flats, and mixed terrain, making it a great choice for everyday riders and enthusiasts alike. With the right knowledge, you can turn that range into real-world comfort, speed, and control.
