Why Safety Gear Matters

Most cycling accidents can be prevented through proper preparation. The right helmet reduces head injury risk by up to 85%, while high-visibility gear and lights make you visible from hundreds of feet away—even in poor lighting conditions.

By taking the time to learn about and invest in quality safety equipment, you're not just protecting yourself—you're enabling years of enjoyable, confident rides ahead.
